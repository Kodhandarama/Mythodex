Rama in remorse asked Sita to return to the palace if she could prove her chastity again before an assembly after banishing her. Sita in anguish cried out "O mother earth just take me away from this place forever!" Whereupon the ground parted the goddess Prithivi arose on a golden throne took Sita in her lap descended and the rift closed. Sita was forever lost.
