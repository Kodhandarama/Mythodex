Maricha assumes the form of a golden deer captivates Sita’s attention. Entranced by the beauty of the deer Sita pleads with Rama to capture it. Rama aware that this is the ploy of the demons cannot dissuade Sita from her desire and chases the deer into the forest leaving Sita under Lakshmana’s guard.
