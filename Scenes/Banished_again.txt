After many months of Rama's rule rumours began to surface among the populace regarding Sita chastity since she had been abducted and imprisoned by a demon. Mindful of the feelings of his subjects Rama banished her. Sita gave birth to her twins Lava an Kusha and they lived in the forest for many years.
