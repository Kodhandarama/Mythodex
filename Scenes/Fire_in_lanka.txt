After meeting with Sita in Ashok Vatika Hanuman wreaks havoc in Lanka by destroying trees and buildings and killing Ravana warriors. He allows himself to be captured and delivered to Ravana. He gives a bold lecture to Ravana to release Sita. He is condemned and his tail is set on fire but he escapes his bonds and leaping from roof to roof sets fire to Ravana's citadel and makes the giant leap back from the island. The joyous search party returns to Kishkindha with the news.
