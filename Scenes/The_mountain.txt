During the battle between Rama’s and Ravana’s army battle Ravana’s son Indrajit hurls a powerful weapon at Lakshmana who is badly wounded. So Hanuman assumes a gigantic form and flies from Lanka to the Himalayas. Upon reaching Mount Sumeru Hanuman was unable to identify the herb that could cure Lakshmana and so decided to bring the entire mountain back to Lanka.
